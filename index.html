Insuring the Future: Creating Solutions for Driverless Automobiles

The future is full of driverless cars, leading to a potential change in how cars are insured. We want you to have an input on how we cover the liabilities that are inherent within automated cars so let’s hear your great ideas for a new insurance product.

Challenge

Are you ready to take up our challenge?

Innovation

The insurance industry is having its Napster moment. Innovations within driverless cars are great for car insurance consumers. The experts report that automated cars will lead to a plunge in accidents, require less coverage and reduce insurance premiums. While this is great news, automated cars are not without risk as the technology behind the automation does introduce the possibility of ill intent by hackers.

View details »

Responsibility

The car insurance industry will need to be redefined to take into account a different type of liability and the shared responsibility. We aim to protect all commuters, but how do we do approach this to give the best value to our customers.

View details »

There is lots to consider when creating a new insurance product for driverless cars. Here are a few contemplations to get you going:

  • Who is liable when an accident happens?
  • How can we protect individuals, companies, families?
  • Who needs insurance – manufacturers, technology suppliers, individuals?
  • How can we create a marketplace that is fair and accessible for everyone?
